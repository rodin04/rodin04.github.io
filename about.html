About Instant pace

About Me

Hi! I'm a student and passionate software developer from Germany.

I love building creative digital solutions and useful tools that make everyday life easier.

Know Your Pace – Instantly!

• Track your runs by simply entering the number of runs you’ve done.

• The tool will then generate rows where you can enter the distance and time for each run.

• After that, it automatically calculates your pace, m/s, km/h, splits for each run, and much more — even your overall averages.

What Makes This Project Unique

• This pace calculator is simple, fast, and easy to use.

• Many other tools have complex layouts or unnecessary features — mine is clean and focused.

• Pace calculators with this level of simplicity and usability are quite rare.

• Whether you're just starting to run or training for a marathon, this tool helps you calculate and understand your running pace without any distractions.

Visions for the Future

• I'm constantly learning and improving.

• I plan to expand this platform with more details and maybe open-source contributions in the future.

Why I Built This Tool

• I built this tool because I couldn’t find a simple, clean, and easy-to-use pace calculator for my own runs — so I decided to make one myself.
